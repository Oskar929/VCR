"Experience the rich and full-bodied coffee blend, roasted to produce a robust flavor with hints of cocoa and nutty notes. This dark roast is meant for those looking for the strong and bold. You’ll taste depth and intensity from the first sip. Suggested brewing type includes standard methods such as drip, French press, and espresso. Recommended both as a hot or iced beverage. Rosca pairs especially well with a classic almond croissant, white chocolate biscotti. or a decadent fudge brownie. Enjoy the taste of Rosca with every sip of this premium dark roast blend.”

Founded in the northwest side of South America lies one of Vancouver Coffee Roaster’s longest partners of fair trade coffee farmers. Our main farmers at Valdez Coffee Farm, focus on growing beans with the best direct care. Starting from the ground, no synthetic fertilizers are used in the process. Instead of relying on chemical pesticides, Valdez specifically uses Diatomaceous Earth as a natural pesticide method. Once the beans are fully ripe, they are harvested and proceed to a selection process. Any unripe or shriveled bean is eliminated. Finally the beans are carried out to production partners within the region to maintain freshness till its final product
